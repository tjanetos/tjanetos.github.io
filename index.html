Current Projects

Geographic Variations of Glaucoma Medications

This project aims to explore the trends in medical treatment of glaucoma among ophthalmologists and optometrists. The project's goals and future work include describing the trends, determining factors that contribute to the geographic and provider variations and determining whether patient outcomes are affected by treatment variations. An initial exploratory study has been submitted to JAMA Ophthalmology.

FDA Class I Recalls

This is a series of projects regarding the FDA regulatory process of medical devices. Current focus of the project is regarding medical field specific recall events and whether certain fields of medicine are more prone to dangerous class I recalls. Additionally, our team focuses on recent legislation including the recently passed 21st Century Cures Act and how legislation affects the regulatory process.
